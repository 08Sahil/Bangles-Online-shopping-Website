Our Services

Name Ordering

Personalized bangles with a name engraved on them are a popular accessory for various special occasions such as parties, functions, and weddings.

Jaipuri Bangles

Jaipuri bangles are traditional Indian bangles that are made in the city of Jaipur. These bangles are a popular accessory for women in India and are often worn to weddings, festivals, and other special occasions.

Three/Single Piece

Three-piece or two-piece bangles are a type of traditional Indian bangles that are sold in sets of two or three. These bangles are a popular accessory for women in India and are often worn to weddings, festivals, and other special occasions.

Kangan Set

Kangan sets are a popular accessory for women in India and are often worn to weddings, festivals, and other special occasions. They can be worn alone or stacked with other bangles to create a layered look.
